WasteX

WasteX, “where the waste product of one process becomes the raw materials for a second process”. WasteX is used for the collection of unwanted wastes like old papers, cardboards, plastics, metal scraps, E-waste from the customer, local collectors and so on. The customer will receive money and earn credits by giving the waste materials. This is similar to using pre-consumer recycling material in a product. This represents a way of reducing waste disposal through reuse for that which cannot be eliminated. In this way WasteX practices are high on the waste hierarchy.
